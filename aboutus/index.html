Rudolph Homes is a locally owned rental company based in Seattle, dedicated to providing high-quality duplex properties and long-term rental options. With a strong focus on community and tenant satisfaction, Rudolph Homes offers comfortable, well-maintained homes in some of Seattle’s most sought-after neighborhoods.

Our portfolio of duplexes is carefully managed to meet the needs of families, professionals, and individuals seeking reliable and welcoming housing. At Rudolph Homes, we prioritize open communication and responsive service, ensuring every tenant feels supported and at home.

As a proud member of the Seattle community, Rudolph Homes understands the dynamics of the local rental market and offers leasing solutions tailored to long-term renters. Whether you’re looking to settle in for a year or establish lasting roots, Rudolph Homes is here to provide a rental experience built on trust, care, and quality.

Welcome to Rudolph Homes—where comfort and community meet.
